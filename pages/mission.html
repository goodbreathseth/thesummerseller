Mission: Our website is built to provide the essential Field Guide to the summer sales experience. We assist potential summer sales reps to:

  1. 1.) Understand how the summer sales industry works.

  2. 2.) Decide which sales track is the best fit (if any at all) for their goals and skill set.

  3. 3.) Identify which company is the best fit for them personally.

  4. 4.) Help them connect with the corresponding companies.
